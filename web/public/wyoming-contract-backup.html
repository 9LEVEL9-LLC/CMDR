Scope of Work for Wyoming Asset Protection Trust Platform (SOW)

This SOW is issued pursuant to the terms and conditions of the Master Services Agreement between nBrain AI, LLC ("Company") and Wyoming Asset Protection Trust / Meuli & Associates ("Client").

Scope Of Work (Exhibit A)

This Statement of Work ("SOW") outlines the services and deliverables for the design, development, and deployment of a Custom Legal Technology Platform consisting of integrated modules for client portal management, matter management CRM, document assembly, compliance monitoring, and AI-powered automation for Wyoming Asset Protection Trust legal practice.

Summary

Wyoming Asset Protection Trust (WAPA), a specialized legal practice focused on Wyoming asset protection trusts, LLC formations, and estate planning services, requires a custom technology platform to scale operations while maintaining white-glove client service. The practice currently relies on a beneficial partnership with CompanySage for registered agent services and generates 4-12 asset protection trust leads per month through WyomingLLCAttorney.com referrals, but lacks control over the client experience and operational automation.

Critical pain points include: (1) no white-label client portal resulting in clients seeing third-party branding, (2) manual entity expiration monitoring leading to missed renewal deadlines, (3) complex questionnaires requiring 30-minute discovery calls for each client intake, (4) no centralized document assembly causing inconsistent document generation, and (5) no proactive client communication or compliance management system.

The proposed platform will serve as WAPA's integrated legal practice management system that syncs with CompanySage data via API while providing complete control over client experience. The system will automate document generation, monitor compliance deadlines through AI-powered agents, streamline client intake, manage matters and workflows, and enable personalized client communications at scale. This positions WAPA to maintain the beneficial CompanySage partnership while building a sellable business asset and eventual pathway to vertical integration as their own registered agent when strategically appropriate.
